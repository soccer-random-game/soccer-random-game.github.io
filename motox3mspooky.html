Moto X3M Spooky: Halloween Bike Adventure

Moto X3M Spooky is a thrilling bike racing game with a Halloween twist. Ride through haunted levels, jump over creepy obstacles, and beat the clock!

How to Play Moto X3M Spooky:

  • Accelerate - Press UP arrow or W key
  • Brake/Reverse - Press DOWN arrow or S key
  • Flip/Tricks - Use LEFT/RIGHT arrows or A/D keys mid-air
  • Reset Bike - Press R if you get stuck

Complete each spooky level as fast as possible while collecting stars for bonuses. Watch out for ghosts, pumpkins, and other Halloween surprises!
