Sand Tetris: A Gravity-Defying Puzzle Adventure

Sand Tetris reinvents the classic block-stacking game with realistic physics and dynamic sand mechanics. Unlike traditional Tetris, pieces behave like real sand particles, creating unpredictable patterns and exciting challenges.

How to Play Sand Tetris

  1. Use arrow keys: Left/Right to move, Down to accelerate, Up to rotate
  2. Watch the sand physics: Blocks crumble and settle naturally
  3. Create stable structures to prevent premature collapses
  4. Clear lines by filling horizontal rows completely
  5. Survive as long as possible as the game speeds up

Sand Tetris offers endless replayability with its unique physics engine. Each game unfolds differently as the sand-like blocks create organic formations. Challenge your friends and climb the leaderboards in this innovative take on a beloved classic!
